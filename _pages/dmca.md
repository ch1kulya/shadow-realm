<h2>Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) Policy</h2>
<p>
    Shadowslave.ru is an online service provider. We provide users with the
    opportunity to place texts from open sources on our site. We do not monitor,
    screen, or otherwise review the content uploaded to our servers by users. We
    take copyright violation seriously and will take action to protect the
    rights of legal copyright owners. If you are the copyright owner of content
    that appears on the shadowslave.ru website without your authorization, you must
    notify us in writing to identify and address the allegedly infringing
    content.
</p>

<h2>II. COMPLAINT NOTICE PROCEDURES FOR COPYRIGHT OWNERS</h2>
<p>
    The following elements must be included in your copyright infringement
    claim:
</p>
<ol>
    <li>
        An electronic or physical signature of the copyright owner or a person
        authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is
        allegedly infringed.
    </li>
    <li>
        Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed,
        or if multiple copyrighted works at a single online site are covered by
        a single notice, a representative list of such works at that site.
    </li>
    <li>
        Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing or to be
        the subject of infringing activity and that is to be removed or access
        to which is to be disabled, and information reasonably sufficient to
        permit the Company to locate the material.
    </li>
    <li>
        Information reasonably sufficient to permit the Company to contact the
        complaining party, such as an address, telephone number, and, if
        available, an electronic mail address at which the complaining party may
        be contacted.
    </li>
    <li>
        A statement that the complaining party has a good faith belief that use
        of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the
        copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
    </li>
    <li>
        A statement that the information in the notice is accurate, and under
        penalty of perjury, that the complaining party is authorized to act on
        behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
    </li>
</ol>
<p>
    Failure to include all of the above information may result in a delay in the
    processing of the DMCA notification.
</p>

<h2>III. NOTICE AND TAKE DOWN PROCEDURE</h2>
<p>
    It is expected that all users of any part of the Company system will comply
    with applicable copyright laws. However, if the Company receives proper
    notification of claimed copyright infringement, it will respond
    expeditiously by removing or disabling access to the material that is
    claimed to be infringing, provided all such claims have been investigated
    and determined to be valid by the Company. The Company will comply with the
    appropriate provisions of the DMCA in the event a counter notification is
    received.
</p>

<h2>IV. REPEAT INFRINGERS</h2>
<p>
    Under appropriate circumstances, the Company may, in its discretion,
    terminate authorization of users of its system or network who are repeat
    infringers.
</p>

<h2>V. ACCOMMODATION OF STANDARD TECHNICAL MEASURES</h2>
<p>
    It is the Company's policy to accommodate and not interfere with standard
    technical measures it determines are reasonable under the circumstances,

</p>
